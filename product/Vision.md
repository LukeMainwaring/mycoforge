# Vision

**The knowledge base is the part you always keep.** Products come and go as
separate sporeforge instances; the KB persists and feeds them.

MycoForge makes the Karpathy-pattern LLM wiki (see
[[Why LLM Wikis Beat RAG]]) a **template you instantiate per domain** — a research
topic, a personal project, a books library, a whole-vault orchestrator — instead
of a thing you hand-build and let diverge. One `gh repo create --template` away
from a working wiki with schema, linter, capture skills, and an MCP feed, plus a
subtractive onboarding that prunes it to exactly your shape and then deletes
itself.

The end state: a vault of federated KBs — each portable, each linting itself,
each one skill away from template updates — quietly compounding while the
software they feed gets rewritten around them.
