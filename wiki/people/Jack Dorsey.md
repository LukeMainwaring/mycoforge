# Jack Dorsey

Co-founder of Twitter and Block. Cited secondhand in both YC talks as the
originator of two [[AI-Native Company]] ideas: that the company must be rebuilt
as an intelligence layer "with humans at the edge guiding it rather than routing
information through it" (his work at Block), and the three employee archetypes —
IC, DRI, AI founder. No primary source in `raw/` yet; his tweets/threads from
early 2026 would be the upstream reference if one is captured.
