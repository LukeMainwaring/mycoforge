# Tom Blomfield

Y Combinator General Partner (founder of Monzo and GoCardless). His May 2026
batch talk — building on [[Diana Hu]]'s and on [[Jack Dorsey]]'s public threads
— supplies this KB's loop anatomy (sensor / policy / tool / quality gate /
learning, see [[Self-Improving AI Loop]]), the [[Company Brain]] with humans at
the edge, the "if it wasn't recorded it didn't happen" legibility principle (see
[[Organizational Legibility]]), and the self-improving YC user manual example.
Rejects Dorsey's third role archetype, keeping only IC and DRI.
